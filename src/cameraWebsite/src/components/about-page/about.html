About MeteorPi

MeteorPi was developed by Cambridge Science Centre as part of its 2015-16 Cosmic Exhibition. The hardware designs and software used by the MeteorPi cameras is all open source, and can be found on our GitHub pages.

Join the MeteorPi Network!

In addition to the activities suggested on this website, you're also welcome to try building your own camera, or to modify the software used to make observations.

You can find a complete guide to how to build your own camera on our GitHub pages.

All of the software that MeteorPi uses is open source, meaning that you're free to download and modify it. If you manage to improve it, we'd be delighted to share your changes with other users!

If you'd like to get involved, why not get in touch with us via Facebook or Twitter?
