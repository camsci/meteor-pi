Using the Region Editor

Selection
Click on the interior of a polygon or on any of its points to select it. Click on an empty region, or on the border, to de-select
Move points
Click and drag to move a point. Hold shift after starting the drag to snap to points of other polygons.
Add points
Click with shift held either near the border of a polygon, or, if a polygon is selected, anywhere on the image to create a new point.
Delete points
Click with shift held on a point to delete it.

Note - where points overlap, points belonging to a selected polygon will be moved or deleted.
